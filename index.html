Who are we?

We are Coder's Cafe Organization aiming to help all kids—including special needs and underrepresented groups—to learn computer science with us!

Our Goals

InclusionTo support interested kids and students like you

We welcome any kids and students who are interested and passionate about computer science to come join with us! People like you are needed more than ever to develop applications to support the changing industry of the dawn of 21st century!

InspireTo foster and grow the interest in computer science

We guide them to learn and colloborate on variety of different, interesting topics and projects. Show them the many cool and creative projects the world has created. And, motivate them to continue their own, epic journey to see what computer science has to show!

InspectTo expose niche fields in computer science

Computer science is not just a field of programming, artificial intelligence (AI), data science, or whatever the media is hyping these days. This field has many niche topics that are useful to the world in their own ways!

Activity Sectors

We separate our activities into three different sectors to balance out the different activities to help members and communities the most.

Educational Activities

To learn new stuff through variety of creative lessons and projects through the topics of that class!

This activity sector is designed through many small lessons subdivided into modules in which we the learner can learn new sub-topics from the main idea of the respective curriculms.

Developmental Activities

To let members experience the colloborative project-based workflow through volunteering to create applications for others!

The purpose of this activity sector is first to allow the members to help the community by volunteering to develop applications for the use of everyone, but also secondly, to allow the members learn what most real-life application development is like.

Event Activities

To host fun competitions inside or outside of the organization that uses the most esoteric and wackiest ideas!

The activity sector that help expand our mission! But also serves the purpose for competetors to compete niche goals (e.g. code golfing) and other more common events like Hackathons and Algorithms.
